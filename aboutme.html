About Me

I am an illustrator from the United States.

I've been drawing since I could hold a pencil. Art is a great way to express yourself, and that's what I do. Sorry, I don't know what to write here, so I'll just write whatever to make it look more full.

My name is Emily and I come from the terrifying city called New York. If you didn't know, New York is located in the United States of America, which is located in North America, which is located on the planet Earth. Anyways the weather here is strange. It can be hot in the winter and it snows in the fall. Wow, it sure is going to take a while to fill up this space. Okay so, let me tell you my life story because that's what I saw on a art blog.
